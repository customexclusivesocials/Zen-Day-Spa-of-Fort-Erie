Our Approach

No two visits are drawn quite the same.

An ensō is drawn in a single breath, and no artist paints the same circle twice. We work the same way — we start with how you're actually feeling that day, not a fixed script, and build the appointment around it.

That might mean a longer facial and a shorter chat, or the other way around. It's a small studio, so it's easy to keep it personal.

About Us

A small studio, run the way a good spa should be.
