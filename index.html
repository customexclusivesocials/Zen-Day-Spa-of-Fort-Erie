Our Approach

Wellness is the whole point, not an add-on.

Every treatment here is built around a simple, holistic idea: your skin, your body and your state of mind are connected, and good care should speak to all three. We ask how you're actually feeling before we ever open a product.

That might mean a quieter facial, a longer soak, or a bit more conversation. It's a small studio, so it stays personal — and it stays gentle.

About Us

A small studio, run the way holistic care should be.

Zen Day Spa of Fort Erie is a locally run day spa on Ridge Road North, built around one idea: that an hour of genuine, whole-person attention is worth more than a long menu of add-ons.
